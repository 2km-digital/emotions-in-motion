About this piece

About Laban Effort Qualities

This piece uses Rudolf Laban's Effort framework to drive procedural animation. Instead of keyframing each emotion by hand, we define four movement qualities — and the engine synthesizes the motion in real time.

The Four Axes
  • Weight Light ↔ Strong
  • Time Sustained ↔ Sudden
  • Space Indirect ↔ Direct
  • Flow Free ↔ Bound
How It Works

Each emotion is a point in this 4D Laban space. When you type an emotion, the blob adopts that point's values. When you drag a slider, the engine finds the nearest canonical emotion and blends its rhythm and direction with your custom Laban values — so the motion always stays coherent.

The blob's surface is a spring-mass system: each point on its skin responds to rhythm envelopes shaped by the Laban dials. Weight controls gravity and scale. Time controls speed and attack. Space controls path curvature. Flow controls damping and overshoot.
